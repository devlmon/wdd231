Games for Your Enjoyment - Site Plan

Luis Eduardo Montenegro Llamozas

Overview

Site Name

Games for Your Enjoyment.

- A personalized game recommendation site that matches players with good games based on their preferences.

Site Purpose

- The site features an interactive form that asks questions about their preferences, mood, and gaming style, then delivers a personalized game recommendation with randomness. The goal is to help players find games that will have a lasting, meaningful impact on them or at least help them have fun and relax for a while.

Scenarios

For Games for Your Enjoyment...

- What game should I play if I only have a few hours this week?

- I want a game with a great story that will really move me. What should I play?

- How can I find games that my friends and I will all enjoy playing together?

- What modern games have that retro feel I love from classic games?
